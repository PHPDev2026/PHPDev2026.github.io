How It Works

A straightforward process from initial contact to delivery—no surprises, just results.

My Process

Initial Discussion

2

Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical turnaround time?

Small bug fixes: same day or next day. Medium features: 1-3 days. Complete applications: 3-7 days depending on complexity. I work fast—what others quote in weeks, I typically deliver in days.

Do you work hourly or project-based?

Both. Hourly (£30-50/hour) for ongoing work or small tasks. Fixed-price for well-defined projects. Typical bug fix costs £60-200, small features £240-600. Retainer arrangements available for consistent monthly work.

What timezone are you in?

UK-based (GMT/BST). I primarily use asynchronous text communication (email, chat) which works well across timezones—we don't need to be online simultaneously.

Can you work with my existing codebase?

Yes. I regularly work on legacy PHP code, inherited projects, and messy codebases. I can debug, refactor, extend, or optimize existing applications regardless of their current state.

Do you work with Laravel specifically?

Yes. For complex applications that need structure and best practices, I use Laravel. For simpler projects, existing code, or quick fixes, I work in standard PHP. The goal is always solving your problem with the right tool.

Do you sign NDAs or work agreements?

Absolutely. I'm happy to sign NDAs, work-for-hire agreements, or any standard contracts. Your code and data remain confidential.

What if I need changes after delivery?

Post-delivery support is included to fix any bugs or issues that arise from my work. Additional features or scope changes beyond the original agreement are quoted separately.

What payment methods do you accept?

PayPal or bank transfer. PayPal preferred as it's safer for both parties and tracks everything. Payment terms discussed during proposal—typically 50% upfront for larger projects, or weekly/biweekly invoices for ongoing work.

What if my project is urgent?

I can accommodate urgent/emergency fixes. Same-day or next-day turnaround is usually possible for critical bugs. Emergency work may be quoted at premium rates depending on the timeline.
